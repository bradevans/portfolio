Examples of other contributions: In 2009, the university agreed to pay for 78 percent of a new transit center estimated to cost as much as $45 million. It also paid more than $1 million to the Ann Arbor Transportation Authority for bus rides taken by university employees and students. In addition, the university contributes the equivalent of $220,000 in lease and maintenance costs by providing and maintaining a fire station on the university campus and helps finance Ann Arbor Spark, a regional economic-development agency, with an annual payment of $350,000.
